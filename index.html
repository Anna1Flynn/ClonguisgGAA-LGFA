About Our Club

Clonguish GAA, established in 1889, is a Gaelic football and hurling club based in Newtownforbes, Co. Longford, Ireland. The club plays at Bertie Allen Park, which offers two full-size pitches, floodlights, dressing rooms and a gym. It holds a proud history of reaching the Leinster Club Championship Semi-Finals twice (1973, 2003). The club is a key part of the local community.
